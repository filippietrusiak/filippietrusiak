## About Me

I lead R&D at Hexagon AB, a global leader in industrial AI, autonomous solutions, and geospatial technology. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and applied mathematics — building perception and analytics systems that run in demanding real-world environments.

I hold a PhD from Politechnika Łódzka, where my dissertation focused on optimization principles for nonlinear monotone operators and their applications to variational and hemivariational problems. This mathematical foundation shapes how I approach ML model design and convergence guarantees.

Before Hexagon, I was a Tech Lead / Data Scientist at Digica, working on CV and ML solutions for industrial clients. Earlier, I spent three years as a C++ developer at Hexagon before transitioning into research.
